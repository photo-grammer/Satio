It astonishes me how much a space influences and affects the people occupying it. The way spaces are connected, and the materials, colours, and light all carry such weight in how we function and operate every day.

I have this passion and desire in me to bring out the potential of a space through combining it with the potential and essence of the people living in these spaces.

Satio Interior Design was born out of a longing to help clients open their eyes to the loud, yet silent voice of their surroundings, enhancing their lives through thoughtfully curated spaces.

My Story

Have you ever paused for a moment just to take note of your surroundings? Where are you sitting now? What do you feel and how does that space around you affect your mood and your lifestyle?

These are the types of things that constantly go through my mind everywhere I go. Being a designer, you get to see these things — you see potential where others neglect. It is all in the finer detail, from how sharp the lighting in a space is to the colours and textures that connect people with a space.

"The word 'satio' is Latin for sowing and a root word for the word seasons. The main goal of Satio Interior Design is to take interior spaces and breathe life into them."
