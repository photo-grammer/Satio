The Philosophy

Sowing Life
Into Every Space

The word 'satio' is Latin for sowing and a root word for the word seasons.

The main goal of Satio Interior Design is to take interior spaces and breathe life into them, so that these spaces can effectively fill the need of the users occupying them.

"We aim to take whatever space is presented and to bring it into a summer season again."

These spaces will in effect add flavour to the surrounding communities and families living in these spaces. At Satio Interior Design, we want to sow back into the lives of our clients by bringing their dreams into reality and creating spaces that enable the user to live out their lives in honesty.

The Designer

Jacolene
Prinsloo

"The heart of Satio Interior Design is to create spaces that inspire, connect, and enhance the lives of the people occupying them."

Based in Pretoria, South Africa, Jacolene Prinsloo has spent over 6 years transforming residential and boutique commercial properties into quiet luxury sanctuaries. Satio was born out of a longing to walk alongside clients on a collaborative journey, translating their visions into physical structure and honest, functional spaces.

Every detail—from the temperature of the lighting to the texture of the materials—is curated with intention to ensure your surroundings speak to who you are.
